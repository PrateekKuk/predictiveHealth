What is Gabapentin?

Category: Prescription Drugs

Most popular types: Neurontin Gabatin Gralise Ratio-Gabapentin APO-Gabapentin DOM-Gabapentin Nupentin Epleptin PMS-Gabapentin Gabaran Show all

false

Gabapentin is an anticonvulsant medication used as an adjunct treatment for partial seizures and for the management of postherpetic neuralgia and neuropathic pain.
