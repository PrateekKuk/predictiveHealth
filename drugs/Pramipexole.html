What is Pramipexole?

Category: Prescription Drugs

Most popular types: Mirapex Mirapex ER Sifrol Sifrol ER Mirapexin Novo-Pramipexole Teva pramipexole Pexola Premipex Show all

false

Pramipexole is an anti-Parkinson's agent that is used to treat symptoms of Parkinson's Disease, such as stiffness, tremors, muscle spasms, and poor muscle control and for moderate to severe primary restless legs syndrome. It may also be used for the treatment of depression.
