What is Quetiapine?

Category: Prescription Drugs

Most popular types: Seroquel Seroquel XR Ratio-Quetiapine Dopaquel Qusel Ketipinor Quetoser Cobalt-Quetiapine Ketilept Show all

false

Quetiapine is an atypical antipsychotics that is used to treat schizophrenia; for manic episodes and maintenance treatment in bipolar I and for depression in bipolar disorder. It may able be used for autism and psychosis/agitation related to Alzheimer's dementia.
